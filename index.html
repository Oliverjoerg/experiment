About Parkacar

Parkacar aims to revolutionize parking by allowing property owners to share their unused parking spaces. Our vision is to create a hassle-free parking experience for everyone while reducing the environmental impact of parking searches. Our team is dedicated to providing innovative solutions and excellent service to our users.

We are committed to sustainability and efficiency. By making better use of existing parking spaces, we help reduce the need for new parking structures and contribute to lower CO2 emissions. Our app also aims to support electric vehicle users by planning to include charging station availability in the future.
